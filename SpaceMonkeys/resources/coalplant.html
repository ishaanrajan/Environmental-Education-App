Coal Plant

Coal plants can produce up to 51,000 MWh daily which ranks 3rd highest of used energy sources. It is also still the most commonly used source of energy in Utah. Although very efficient, coal plants do leave an environmental impact. They are the most costly energy source. On average a coal plant will produce 1500 gCO2/kWh during its entire life cycle.
